Interview Question

  1. Are you interested in becoming more financially literate?
  2. Do you currently have access to financial literacy resources?
  3. Rate your financial awareness on the following topics from 1-10
    1. Budgeting
    2. Investing
    3. Retirement
    4. Cryptocurrency
    5. Credit
    6. Loans
  4. Do you have access to an application that provides you with budgeting, investing, or retirement tools?
  5. How often do you currently use such tools?
  6. Would you use them more frequently if they were convenient to access and use?
  7. Would you be interested in a web application that provides easy access to budgeting tools, as well as all necessary resources to assist you in becoming more financially literate?
